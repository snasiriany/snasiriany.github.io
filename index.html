Soroush Nasiriany

I am a CS PhD student at UT Austin, advised by Professor Yuke Zhu at the Robot Perception and Learning Lab. I'm also spending time as a Student Researcher at Google DeepMind. Previously, I recieved my undergraduate and master's degrees from UC Berkeley, where I was advised by Professor Sergey Levine.

I'm interested in building intelligent robot agents that can perform useful behaviors in diverse real world settings. Recently I've been working on harnessing diverse sources of data, namely internet data, robot simulation and real world robot data, to build a powerful foundation model for robotics.
