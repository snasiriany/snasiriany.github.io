Soroush Nasiriany

I am a second year CS PhD student at UT Austin, advised by Professor Yuke Zhu at the Robot Perception and Learning Lab. I'm working towards developing versatile robot agents that can be deployed in the real world, using tools from machine learning, cognitive science, and computer vision.

Previously, I recieved my undergraduate and master's degrees from UC Berkeley, where I was advised by Professor Sergey Levine. During my time at Berkeley, I helped develop the curriculum for CS 189, the machine learning course.
